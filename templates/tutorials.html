CREATE AN ACCOUNT

To create an account:

  1. Click on the sign up button
  2. Enter your details into the provided fields.
  3. Click on the Sign Up button
  4. Welcome on board! You will be redirected to the homepage

If you already have an account login here

LOGIN TO YOUR ACCOUNT

If you already have an account:

  1. Click on the login button
  2. Enter your email address and password
  3. Click on the login button
  4. You will be redirected to the homepage where you can start designing

START DESIGNING

To start creating your custom card design:

  1. Click on the libraries button on the navigation bar
  2. Select any card design of your choice
  3. Click on the design
  4. Type your own details into the provided form
  5. Click download and your design will be automatically downloaded

RESET YOUR PASSWORD

To reset your password:

  1. Click on the profile icon on the right corner of your navigation bar
  2. Click on settings from the options that pop up
  3. Click on change password from the settings options
  4. Enter your email address
  5. A reset link will be sent to your email

LOGOUT OF YOUR ACCOUNT

To logout of your account:

  1. Click on the profile icon on the right corner of your navigation bar
  2. Click on settings from the options that pop up
  3. At the buttom of the settings page is the sign out button
  4. You will be redirected to the landing page
